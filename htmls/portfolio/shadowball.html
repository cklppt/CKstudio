Project

ShadowBall

A Human-Computer Interaction game controlled via Body Movement and Gestures using Microsoft Kinect. Players use their silhouette to control and reflect the balls through desired path and put them into correct holes.

Developed in C#, XNA and farseer physics engine.

The design process is running Design-Implement-Analysis cycle with various prototypes.

Keywords: HCI, Kinect, Montion Capture, DIA cycle, physics engine.
