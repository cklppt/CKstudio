BeGreen Multiuser Eco-driving Simulator

A racing-game alike multiuser driving simulator which helps people to drive in an Eco-friendly manner. Implemented the driving and eco feedback interface. Mainly focus on developing car and driving related feature.

The system has been actul deployed and tested in a class of 40 junior high students playing simultaneously as part of ECO-lesson teaching.

Developed in C# and Unity3D engine.

Keywords: C#, Unity, Interface Design
