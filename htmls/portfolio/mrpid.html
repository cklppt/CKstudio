Project

Mixed-Reality Presentation for Interior Designers

Stereoscopic-aided Mixed-Reality Presentation for Interior Designers, concept design of a platform/interface aiming at helping interior designers better present their ideas to customers.

In our design, we incorporate 3D glasses(head-mounted display), projectors, gesture recognition and internal software to help visualizing the image in the designer's head to customers.

The design process is running Design-Implement-Analysis cycle with various prototypes.

Design concept: storyboard Ver2.0 Ver3.0 Ver4.0

Keywords: HCI, AR/VR/Mixed-Reality, Head-mounted display, Interaction Design
